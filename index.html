About Me

I'm a Computer Engineering student with a passion for bridging the gap between hardware and software. I love working on systems where both worlds collide writing firmware for microcontrollers, designing PCBs, and building the software layers that make everything talk together.

Outside of coursework, I tinker with Arduino and ESP32 projects, explore PC hardware optimization, and build tools that solve real problems. I believe the best engineers understand the full stack — from transistors to user interfaces.

This portfolio documents the work I've done throughout my studies a live record of projects, experiments, and everything in between.
